🐕 Dog Vaccines

CORE
🛡️

Rabies

Mandatory by law. Protects against the fatal rabies virus, transmissible to humans. Given at 12–16 weeks, then annually.

CORE

DHPP

Combination vaccine covering Distemper, Hepatitis, Parvovirus & Parainfluenza — the four most dangerous canine diseases.

OPTIONAL
💧

Leptospirosis

Recommended for dogs exposed to water, soil or wildlife. Also protects humans (zoonotic disease).

OPTIONAL
🌬️

Kennel Cough (Bordetella)

Recommended for dogs that visit parks, groomers, or boarding facilities. Prevents highly contagious respiratory infection.

🐈 Cat Vaccines

CORE
🛡️

FVRCP

Covers Feline Viral Rhinotracheitis, Calicivirus & Panleukopenia — essential for all cats regardless of lifestyle.

CORE
🦠

Rabies

Required for all cats. Protects against rabies virus and is legally mandated in most regions.

📅 Vaccination Schedule (Dogs)

6–8 weeks

First DHPP vaccine

10–12 weeks

Second DHPP + Leptospirosis (if needed)

14–16 weeks

Third DHPP + Rabies vaccine

1 year

Booster for all core vaccines

Every year after

Annual boosters to maintain immunity

⚠️ What We Monitor

👁️

Pre-Vaccine Health Check

We examine your pet before every vaccination to ensure they are healthy enough to receive the vaccine safely.

⏱️

Post-Vaccine Observation

We monitor for 15–20 minutes after injection for any immediate reactions like swelling or lethargy.
