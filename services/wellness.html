Wellness Exams

Comprehensive preventive health evaluations to keep your pet thriving year-round

Book an Exam

🔍 What's Included

A full-body preventive health evaluation covering every aspect of your pet's wellbeing:

Weight, temperature, heart rate & respiration
Eyes, ears, nose & throat examination
Skin & coat condition (ticks, fleas, allergies)
Teeth & gums check
Abdomen palpation (liver, kidneys, organs)
Joint & mobility assessment
Behavioral observation

🧪 Optional Diagnostic Tests

🩸

Blood Tests

CBC (Complete Blood Count) and biochemistry panel to assess organ function and detect infections.

Urine Analysis

Detects kidney disease, diabetes, urinary tract infections and other metabolic conditions.

🦠

Stool Exam

Screens for intestinal parasites like roundworms, hookworms, giardia and coccidia.

🕷️

Disease Screening

Tick fever, heartworm, and other region-specific disease panels for Ludhiana's climate.

🎯 Why It Matters

01

Early Detection

Growth Monitoring
